about

Mamboleo FC is a non-profit organization that promotes physical and mental health, diversity and inclusion through our soccer community. With the help of our volunteer and player community, we provide free soccer training, organized league play, and assistance to those experiencing financial barriers to participating in soccer.

At the heart of our club is a common love for the beautiful game and a desire to constantly improve our skills, fitness and teamwork, regardless of age!

We promote culture, diversity and inclusion by bringing together individuals of every background imaginable under the shared values of kindness, respect and openness.

Our philosophy is that connection is the antidote to depression and that when we expose ourselves to new experiences and new people - our worlds can grow exponentially.
