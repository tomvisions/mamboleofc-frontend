The Flamingo

The flamingo is one of the most distinctive and recognizable birds of the animal kingdom, with its long neck, exotic pink plumage, curved beak and gangly legs. They can be found on nearly every continent, including North and South America, southern Europe, Africa and parts of Asia. They love to congregate in large groups, where they have massive dance parties as part of their courtship routine.

Looks and social life aside, this bird is extremely tough, and can survive conditions ranging from sub-zero temperatures to boiling geysers and hypersaline lakes that could strip away human skin!
